## Короткий ответ

Discovery → Requirements → Design → Development → Testing → Release → Support. Важно: ownership, accountability, quality at each stage.

## Контекст

Проверяют понимание полного цикла разработки иownership.

## Как строить ответ

### Discovery

Как взаимодействуете с аналитикой.

### Requirements

Какtransliterate-аете requirements в technical tasks.

### Development

Какorganize-аете development process.

### Testing

Какorganize-аете testing.

### Release

Какorganize-аетеrelease process.

### Support

Какorganize-аетеpost-release support.

## Пример ответа

Discovery: meetings саналитикой,理解 requirements. Requirements: translation в technical tasks, estimation. Development: sprint planning, daily, code review. Testing: unit, integration, QA. Release: CI/CD, blue/green deploy. Support: monitoring, incident response. Результат: full ownership, quality delivery.

## Частые ошибки

- Not owning full cycle
- Not communicating with аналитикой
- Not testing足够
- Not monitoring post-release
