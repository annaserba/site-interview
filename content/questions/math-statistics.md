## Короткий ответ

Mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation, correlation, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, p-value, A/B testing.

## Контекст

Фундаментальная статистика для data analysis. Проверяют понимание statistical concepts.

## Как строить ответ

### Descriptive statistics

Mean: average. Median: middle value. Mode: most frequent. Variance/SD: spread.

### Inferential statistics

Hypothesis testing: H₀ vs H₁. P-value: probability of observing data given H₀. Confidence interval: range for true parameter.

### A/B testing

Compare two variants. Statistical significance: p-value < 0.05. Power: probability of detecting effect.

## Пример ответа

Mean: (1+2+3+4+5)/5 = 3. Variance: E[(X-μ)²]. Correlation: -1 to 1. A/B test: conversion A=10%, B=12%, p=0.03 → B is better.

## Частые ошибки

- Путать correlation и causation
- Не понимать p-value
- Использовать mean для skewed data
- Игнорировать confidence intervals
