## Короткий ответ

Components, tokens, documentation, Storybook. Важно: consistency, reusability, accessibility.

## Контекст

Проверяют понимание design systems.

## Как строить ответ

### Components

Atomic design: atoms, molecules, organisms.

### Tokens

Colors, typography, spacing.

### Documentation

Storybook, guidelines.

### Accessibility

WCAG compliance, testing.

## Пример ответа

Components: Button, Input, Card (atoms). Tokens: colors, typography, spacing. Documentation: Storybook, props, examples. Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA. Результат: consistency, faster development.

## Частые ошибки

- Not documenting
- Not versioning
- Not accessible
- Not reusable
