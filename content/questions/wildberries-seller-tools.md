## Короткий ответ

Seller tools: analytics dashboard, inventory management, pricing tools, advertising, order processing. Ключевые: real-time data, actionable insights, automation.

## Контекст

Ключевой feature для привлечения sellers. Проверяют понимание seller ecosystem.

## Как строить ответ

### Analytics

Sales metrics: revenue, orders, conversion. Traffic: views, clicks, CTR. Competitor analysis.

### Inventory management

Stock levels. Reorder alerts. Multi-warehouse support.

### Pricing tools

Dynamic pricing. Competitor price monitoring. Discount management.

## Пример ответа

Dashboard: sales today: 50 orders, revenue: 150,000₽. Analytics: conversion 3%, average order 3,000₽. Inventory: 3 items low stock → alert. Pricing: competitor sold for less → adjust.

## Частые ошибки

- Не показывать real-time data
- Игнорировать actionable insights
- Не делать automation
- Не учитывать seller feedback
